How it works — 4 steps to full automation
1
Connect: Enter your broker API key and access token above. For Upstox: generate from upstox.com/developer. For Zerodha: use kite.zerodha.com/connect. NANDI stores credentials locally in your browser only — never transmitted to any third party.
2
Schedule: Set the daily fetch time to 16:05–16:15 IST (after NSE closes at 15:30, after exchange data propagates). NANDI uses the browser timer to trigger at that exact time every trading day.
3
Auto-run: At the scheduled time, NANDI fetches 250 days of OHLC for your selected universe, runs the full signal engine, resolves outcomes in the ledger, recalibrates weights, rebuilds portfolios, and generates anomaly alerts — all automatically. Estimated time: 15–30 seconds.
4
Review: Open NANDI any time after market close. All signals, anomalies, portfolio P&L updates, and weight recalibration are complete. The tab must remain open for scheduled fetches. For fully unattended operation, keep NANDI open on a dedicated screen or tablet.

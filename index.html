Layer 2: Signal Sequence Learning — prev→current chain win rates
Standalone B2 may have 52% WR. B2 that follows B1-OS within 5 bars may have 74% WR. NANDI tracks every prev→current signal pair from the ledger and computes its own win rate. The SeqWR column shows the win rate for that specific chain.
Layer 3: Regime Transition Scoring — signals discounted near regime flips
Within REGIME_TRANS_BARS=5 bars of a bear→bull or bull→bear flip, signal scores are discounted toward neutral (×0.6 to ×1.0 linearly fading). This prevents false entries during regime confirmation.

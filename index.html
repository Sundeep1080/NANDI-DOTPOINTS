Options Spreads for Sell Signals — Bear Call Spread + Bear Put Spread
For each sell signal, NANDI recommends the optimal options spread strategy based on the signal type:
S1-OB / S2 (overbought reversal) → Bear Call Spread: Sell ATM Call + Buy OTM Call. Max profit = net premium received. Risk defined.
S1-MOM / S3 (momentum down) → Bear Put Spread: Buy ATM Put + Sell OTM Put. Debit strategy. Profits on sharp downmove.
Strike selection: ATM ± 1 standard deviation (based on HV × DTE). Weekly expiry preferred.

Portfolio Self-Learning — adaptive score adjustments over time
After each portfolio dissolves, NANDI updates the win rates for each signal type, day of week, and sector. These updated win rates feed back into the scoring engine — signals with higher empirical WR get higher base scores, signals with lower WR get reduced scores. The system becomes more accurate with each week of data.
